Welcome to FUFA Super League Members SACCO

Overview

We are a savings and credit cooperative registered by the Uganda micro finance regulatory authority. It was established in the year 2020.

An affiliate of the FUFA super league (Uganda premier league) with the aim of providing both financial and non-financial services to its members, particularly in the football sector in Uganda.
